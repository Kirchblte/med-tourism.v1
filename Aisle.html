About the Highway

The Aisle of Medellin is a scenic, 7.5-kilometer, 4-lane straight highway in northern Cebu, Philippines, running from Brgy. Dayhagon to Curva.

Known as a "highway of dreams" it features stunning, uninterrupted views of expansive sugarcane fields, making it a top destination for travelers, bikers, and riders.

Location: Brgy. Dayhagon to Curva, Medellin, Cebu
Length: 7.5 kilometers
Highlights: Sugarcane field views, biking, scenic drives
