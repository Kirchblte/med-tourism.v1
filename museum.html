About the Museum

The Museo de Medellin, located in a restored early-1900s ancestral house in the heart of Medellin, Cebu, displays the town's history, focusing on its sugar industry, local traditions, and cultural heritage.

It features vintage agricultural tools, religious artifacts, and old photos, offering a glimpse into the municipality's rural past.

Location: Heart of Medellin, Cebu
Building: Restored early-1900s ancestral house
Highlights: Vintage tools, religious artifacts, historical photos
