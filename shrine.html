Immaculate Conception Shrine

A Sanctuary of Peace and Devotion

About the Shrine

The Shrine of the Mary Immaculate Conception in Brgy. Caputatan Norte, Medellin, Cebu, Philippines, is a developing place of worship and a popular site for devotion to Mama Mary.

Situated in Northern Cebu, it serves as a peaceful prayer site often visited by pilgrims to pray for protection and blessings.

Location: Brgy. Caputatan Norte, Medellin, Cebu
Dedicated to: Mama Mary (Immaculate Conception)
Highlights: Peaceful prayer site, pilgrim destination
