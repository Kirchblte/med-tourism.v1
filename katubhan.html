Katubhan Festival

A Vibrant Thanksgiving Celebration

📷

About the Festival

The Katubhan Festival is an annual, vibrant thanksgiving celebration in Medellin, Cebu, honoring Señor Sto. Niño for a bountiful sugarcane harvest.

Formerly the Medellin Sinulog Festival, it features street dancing, ritual showdowns, and costumes inspired by the sugar industry ("tubo" means sugarcane). It is a major participant in the Pasigarbo sa Sugbo.

Location: Medellin, Cebu, Philippines
Best Time: Annual celebration
Highlights: Street dancing, ritual showdowns, colorful costumes
