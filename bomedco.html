About BOMEDCO

Bogo-Medellin Milling Company, Inc. (BOMEDCO), established in 1928 in Barangay Luy-a, Medellin, Cebu, is a major sugar manufacturer and agricultural company.

It operates a 3,000 tons-per-day mill, producing raw sugar and molasses. Despite temporary milling suspensions due to cane shortages, farming operations continue.

Location: Barangay Luy-a, Medellin, Cebu
Established: 1928
Capacity: 3,000 tons per day
Products: Raw sugar and molasses
