Bamboo Forest

Serenity in Every Step

About the Forest

The Medellin Bamboo Forest in Barangay Luy-a, Northern Cebu, is a popular, man-made, 2.3-hectare tourist attraction featuring dense, towering bamboo groves designed to mimic Kyoto's Sagano Forest.

It offers serene, shaded pathways, providing a peaceful retreat from city life with a relaxing, natural soundscape and affordable photo-worthy spots for nature lovers.

Location: Barangay Luy-a, Medellin, Cebu
Size: 2.3 hectares
Highlights: Towering bamboo groves, shaded pathways, photo spots
